Stripe support is still in beta. Follow @stripe and @tempo on Twitter to learn when it goes live.
:::

The Stripe payment method enables traditional payment rails through [Stripe](https://stripe.com)—credit cards, bank transfers, and invoices.

## Overview

Stripe integration brings familiar payment infrastructure to MPP:

- **Cards** — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and more
- **Bank transfers** — ACH, SEPA, wire transfers
- **Invoices** — Net-30/60/90 billing for enterprise clients
- **Global coverage** — 135+ currencies in 46 countries

## Intents

| Intent | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `charge` | One-time card or bank payment |
